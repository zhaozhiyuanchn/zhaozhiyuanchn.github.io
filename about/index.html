Recent Project

Preparation of Binary Platinum-free Alloy Counter Electrodes and Property Study on Their Dye- sensitized Solar Cells(DSSC) 12/2014-06/2015
Introduced a series of transition metals with different numbers of electrons to Fe and Ru by a low- temperature reduction approach to prepare binary alloy counter electrodes(CEs)
Compared the electrochemical properties of different CEs and its DSSCs, determined the effect law of different numbers of electrons on the catalytic, and explored the catalytic mechanism of alloy CEs afterwards.
Counter Electrodes from Mo-Se Nanosheet Alloys for Bifacial Dye-Sensitized Solar Cells 06/2014-11/2014
Synthesized molybdenum selenide(Mo-Se) binary nanosheet alloys by a mild solution method and employed as CE materials for DSSCs
Obtained a series of transparent Mo-Se alloy CEs which can realize electricity generation from either side. Recorded the maximum front and rear efficiencies of 8.05% and 5.92% under simulated air mass 1.5 global irradiation, respectively
